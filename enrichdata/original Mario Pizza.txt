1. Company Overview
1.1 Business Description

1.1.1 Mario’s Pizza is a locally owned quick-service restaurant located in Toronto, Ontario.

1.1.2 The business specializes in freshly prepared pizzas, sides, and beverages available for delivery and pickup.

1.1.3 The restaurant serves customers within a 10 km radius of its physical location.

1.1.4 Mario’s Pizza focuses on affordability, quick preparation times, and customizable menu options suitable for individuals and families.

4.1.4 Free delivery applies to orders over $30.00.

4.1.5 Estimated delivery time: 30–45 minutes.

4.1.7 Apartment or buzzer number is recommended when applicable.

4.2 Pickup

4.2.1 Pickup orders are typically ready within 15–20 minutes.

4.2.2 There is no minimum order for pickup.
